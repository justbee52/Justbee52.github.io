Why Choose Raw, Unpasteurized Honey?

Our honey is raw and unpasteurized, meaning we preserve all of nature's beneficial enzymes, antioxidants, and nutrients that are typically destroyed during commercial heat processing. This commitment to maintaining honey in its natural state means you receive the maximum health benefits that our bees intended.

Raw honey may naturally crystallize over time â€“ this is actually a sign of quality and purity! Crystallization occurs more readily in raw honey because the natural sugars and particles that commercial processing removes are still present.

The beauty of honey: it never expires. Your JustBee honey will remain perfectly good forever when stored properly. If crystallization occurs, simply place the jar in a warm water bath and gently heat until it returns to its liquid golden state.

About JustBee

At JustBee, we operate from a single apiary location where every drop of our honey is carefully crafted by nature's finest workers. Our philosophy is simple: healthy bees make the best possible honey.

We prioritize the health and wellbeing of our bees above all else, using sustainable and bee-friendly practices that allow our colonies to thrive naturally. This commitment to bee health results in exceptionally pure, flavorful honey that reflects the true essence of what nature intended.

Our honey is raw and lightly filtered to preserve all the natural enzymes, antioxidants, and beneficial compounds while removing only the largest particles. We never add anything artificial â€“ no preservatives, no additives, just pure honey exactly as our bees made it.

Every jar represents the dedication to quality that comes from focusing on one thing and doing it exceptionally well: producing the finest raw honey while keeping our bees happy and healthy.
